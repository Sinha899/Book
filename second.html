The Three Pigs

Once upon a time, there were three little pigs who left their mother to build their own houses.

The first pig built his house out of straw, the second pig built his house out of sticks , and the third pig built his house out of bricks.

A big bad wolf came and blew down the houses of the first two pigs,

but he couldnt blow down the house of the third pig. The wolf tried to enter the house through the chimney, but the third pig had a pot of boiling water on the fire,and the wolf fell into it and died.
